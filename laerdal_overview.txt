Laerdal Medical — Sample Reference Document (for RAG demo)

About
Laerdal Medical is a world leader in healthcare education and resuscitation
training. Its products and services are used by hospitals, ambulance services,
first aid organizations, and educational institutions around the world. The
company's stated goal is to help save one million more lives every year by 2030.

Patient Simulators
Laerdal develops advanced patient simulators used to train healthcare
professionals. These simulators contain sensors and actuators that mimic human
physiological responses such as breathing, blinking, talking, and movement,
allowing realistic, hands-on training without risk to real patients.

CPR Training
Effective cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) requires chest compressions at a
rate of 100 to 120 compressions per minute, at a depth of about 5 to 6
centimeters for an adult. Laerdal manikins provide real-time feedback on
compression rate and depth so learners can correct their technique.

Quality and Safety
Laerdal applies rigorous verification and testing to its products. Background
checks (via Semac) are part of the company's recruitment process, reflecting the
care taken across a regulated medical-device environment.

Locations
Laerdal's headquarters are in Stavanger, Norway, with offices and collaboration
across many countries and cultures.
